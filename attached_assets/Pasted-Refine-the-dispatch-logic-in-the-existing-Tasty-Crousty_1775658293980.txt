CORE DISPATCH RULE
==================================================

A driver should be restricted by **wilaya**, not permanently locked to only one zone.

This means:
- when a driver registers, they select their main wilaya
- they may also have preferred zones
- but during live operations, dispatch must primarily use the driver’s **current location inside that wilaya**

Example:
- if a driver registered in Alger wilaya
- and originally selected Bab Ezzouar
- but after completing a delivery they are now physically in Bordj El Kiffan
- then they should be able to receive nearby orders from Bordj El Kiffan
- they should NOT be limited forever to Bab Ezzouar only
